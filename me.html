About me:

Hi, it's Natvandrer. I do webcoding, as well as art & desing, compose music and anything that my mind finds interesting at a given moment.

Creativity has always been my favourite. Surprisingly, the thing that drives me the most is the making of stuff. The discovery of something new teases me to get my mind involved. It's what made me progress in many various domains for sure!

No matter what I'm working on, I'm trying to be meticulous and pushing the limits to reach my best.

When not being submerged in schoolwork, spending time on personal projects is one of the first things I do, whether it is a completely new project or an existing one.

Sharing my work with friends and people is definitely a plus: I'd say I'm pretty perseverant when it comes to dedication, but I'll admit, having positive feedback always boosts the mood! :)
